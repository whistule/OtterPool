About this trip

A classic two-night sea kayaking expedition taking in some of the most dramatic scenery on the west coast. We'll paddle from Kentallen south through the Lynn of Lorn, making our first camp on the island of Shuna before rounding the headland past Castle Stalker on Sunday.

Sunday night camp is at Macdonald's Burial Isle — a remote and beautiful spot accessible only by water. The return journey on Monday takes us through the narrows and back north to Kentallen.

This is a serious Grade C trip requiring solid open water experience and confidence in tidal conditions. A lengthy shuttle is involved — details in the car sharing section below.
